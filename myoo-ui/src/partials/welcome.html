/myoo/ is a project and achievement tracking web application. It's intended to be a fun, gamified way to track your progress on various goals. You can see how well you're doing and even compare your status with other users.

Getting started

You'll want to subscribe to some projects. Use the arrow in the top right corner and choose Manage Projects to find something you're interested in. Once you get the gist of what /myoo/ is about, you can try your hand at creating your own projects.
